About AI Verticals

Who We Are

AI Verticals is an independent editorial publication dedicated to tracking how artificial intelligence is transforming industries. Founded by a team of AI analysts and technology researchers, we cut through the hype to deliver concrete, actionable insights about real-world AI adoption.

Our Mission

No fluff, no hype — just evidence-based analysis of AI applications across eight key industries:

  • Finance: Algorithmic trading, fraud detection, credit scoring, robo-advisors
  • Healthcare: Diagnostics, drug discovery, patient care automation
  • Legal: Contract analysis, legal research, compliance automation
  • Education: Adaptive learning, automated grading, personalized tutoring
  • Manufacturing: Predictive maintenance, quality control, supply chain optimization
  • Retail: Personalization, inventory management, customer behavior analysis
  • Human Resources: Recruitment automation, employee engagement, workforce analytics
  • Media: Content generation, recommendation engines, audience analytics

Our Approach

Every article we publish focuses on specific use cases, real products, and measurable outcomes. We track which companies are actually deploying AI, how they're doing it, and what results they're achieving. When we make predictions, we ground them in current data and cite our sources.
