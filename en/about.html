About AI Verticals

Expert analysis of artificial intelligence transforming industries — backed by real company data and rigorous editorial review.

Our Mission

AI Verticals was founded in 2025 with a clear mission: to provide in-depth, data-driven analysis of how artificial intelligence is transforming industries across the global economy. We believe that understanding AI's real-world applications — beyond the hype and the headlines — is essential for businesses, investors, policymakers, and curious minds alike.

Every day, breakthrough AI research emerges from laboratories, startups, and tech giants. But the gap between a research paper and real-world impact is vast. Our team bridges that gap, analyzing not just what the technology can do in theory, but what it's actually doing in practice — which companies are succeeding, which are failing, and what the data tells us about where the industry is heading.

Our Editorial Process

At AI Verticals, we combine the scale of artificial intelligence with the judgment of human editors. Our editorial process works like this:

1. AI-Assisted Research

Each article begins with comprehensive research assisted by large language models. Our AI systems analyze thousands of sources — academic papers, company filings, news reports, and industry analyses — to synthesize the current state of knowledge on a topic.

2. Human Editorial Review

Every article then undergoes human editorial review. Our editors verify factual claims, check citations, ensure logical coherence, and add the critical context that only human judgment can provide. The result is content that harnesses AI's scale while maintaining editorial integrity.

3. Continuous Updates

AI and its applications evolve rapidly. We continuously monitor our articles for outdated information and update them as new data emerges. Articles display a "last updated" date to ensure transparency about content freshness.

AI-Assisted Research & Editorial Review by AI Verticals Team
All articles on this site are researched and drafted with the assistance of large language models, then reviewed and edited by our editorial team. This hybrid approach allows us to cover more ground with greater depth than traditional editorial teams, while maintaining the accuracy and nuance that our readers expect.

Our Coverage Areas

We provide in-depth analysis across eight key industry verticals:

  • Finance & Banking: From algorithmic trading to fraud detection, AI is reshaping how money moves through the global economy.
  • Healthcare & MedTech: Drug discovery, medical imaging, clinical decision support — AI's impact on human health is profound and growing.
  • Legal Services: Contract review, legal research, e-discovery — AI is augmenting (and in some cases automating) legal work.
  • Education & EdTech: Adaptive learning, AI tutoring, automated assessment — personalizing education at scale.
  • Manufacturing: Predictive maintenance, quality control, supply chain optimization — Industry 4.0 in action.
  • Retail & E-commerce: Recommendation engines, inventory optimization, personalized marketing — AI as the retail engine.
  • Human Resources: Resume screening, people analytics, workforce planning — AI in the people business.
  • Media & Content: Content generation, deepfake detection, news integrity — AI's double-edged role in information.

Our Commitment to Accuracy

We strive for accuracy in every article we publish. When we make errors, we correct them promptly and transparently. If you spot an error in our content, please contact us — we value your feedback and will investigate all reported issues.

Our articles include links to primary sources — academic papers, company reports, regulatory filings — so that readers can verify claims and explore topics in greater depth. We believe in transparent, attributable journalism.
