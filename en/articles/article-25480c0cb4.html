What is Posthorn?

Posthorn is a self-hosted email service that enables users to manage their emails without relying on a third-party mail server. This means that you can host your email service on your own server or cloud space, giving you full control over your email data and privacy. It's like owning a piece of the internet, right down to your inbox!

The Privacy Game-Changer

In an age where data breaches and privacy invasions are the norm, Posthorn offers a refreshing change of pace. By removing the need for a mail server, users can enjoy a more secure email experience. With traditional email services, your data travels through various servers, making it more vulnerable to interception and hacking. Posthorn's self-hosted nature ensures that your emails stay secure on your own terms.
