FAQ Section

What is a public domain image archive?

A public domain image archive is a collection of images that are not protected by copyright and are freely available for use by anyone. These images can range from historical photographs to modern works of art.

How does AI improve the user experience in public domain image archives?

AI improves the user experience by making it easier to search, sort, and analyze images. It can also enhance the accessibility of these archives, making them more accessible to people with disabilities.
