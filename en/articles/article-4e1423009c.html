The Break from Tradition

For years, Canada has relied on the United States for its military plane needs. The switch to Sweden, with its Gripen fighter jets, marks a significant departure from this long-standing relationship. Many Canadians are questioning why this change now, and what it signifies for their country's defense capabilities.
