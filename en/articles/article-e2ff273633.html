The Power of Autodiff

So, what's the big deal about autodiff? Autodiff, short for automatic differentiation, is a method used to compute the derivative of a function. In the context of Geomatic, it means that users can perform computations that would typically require complex mathematical formulas, all with a few simple commands. This not only saves time but also reduces the likelihood of human error.
