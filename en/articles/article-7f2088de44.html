Q: What is GPU capacity, and why is it wasted?

A: GPU capacity refers to the processing power of graphics processing units. Wasted GPU capacity occurs when GPUs are not being used to their full potential, often during off-peak hours or when not in use by their primary owner.

Q: How does Expanse benefit the environment?

A: By harnessing idle GPU capacity, Expanse reduces the need for additional data centers, which consume significant amounts of electricity. This helps lower carbon emissions and conserve energy.

Q: What are the limitations of using idle GPU capacity?
