Why Did This Happen? The Deeper Reason

The root of this issue lies in the relentless pursuit of big data and the rapid advancement of AI technology. Companies are constantly seeking to gather as much information as possible to refine their algorithms and improve user experiences. However, this data-driven frenzy has often crossed ethical lines, leading to privacy breaches and data misuse.

The impact on regular people is significant. Our personal data is more vulnerable than ever before. But there's hope. By becoming more informed and vigilant about the data we share, we can take control of our privacy.
