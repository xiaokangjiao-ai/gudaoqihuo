Fingers: The Key to Gemini's Success

Fingers are the digital keys that allow users to access Gemini's content. Unlike traditional HTTPS keys, Fingers are designed to be more secure and easier to manage. FAQ Section

#### ### What is Gemini?

#### ### How does Gemini differ from HTTPS?
Gemini uses a peer-to-peer architecture, providing end-to-end encryption and enhanced privacy compared to the client-server model of HTTPS.
