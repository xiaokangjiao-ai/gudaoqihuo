Understanding Query Hints in Postgres 19

Query hints are like secret sauce for your database queries. They are instructions given to the database engine to guide its query execution plan. In Postgres 19, these hints are powered by AI, making them more intelligent and adaptable than ever before. But why the sudden surge in AI integration?
