The Legacy of Li Bai

A Brief History of the Poet

Li Bai, or Li Po, was a Chinese poet from the Tang Dynasty, known for his exquisite poetry and love for wine. Born in 701 AD, he is celebrated as one of the greatest poets in Chinese history. His works often depicted scenes of nature, friendship, and his love for wine. The phrase "a cup of wine and a moon to share" is famously attributed to him, reflecting his deep connection with both.
