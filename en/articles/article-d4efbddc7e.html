**Chengdu University Custodian Achieves Postgraduate Admission Succ...

📅 2026-05-20 🔥 Trending

Introduction

In a remarkable story of perseverance and determination, a 41-year-old dormitory custodian from Chengdu University has successfully passed the postgraduate entrance examination. This achievement not only highlights the individual's commitment to education but also serves as an inspiration to others pursuing their dreams at any age.

The Journey Begins

The custodian, surnamed Li, has been working at Chengdu University for over a decade. Despite his busy schedule and the challenges of managing a dormitory, Li had always harbored a dream of pursuing higher education. "Ever since I was young, I've been fascinated by the world of knowledge," Li said in an interview. "I wanted to be a part of it, even if it meant starting late."

The Decision to Take the Leap

Li's decision to take the postgraduate entrance examination was not an easy one. "I was worried about the time commitment and the possibility of failing," he admitted. However, his passion for learning and the support of his family motivated him to take the leap. "I told myself that if I didn't try, I would always regret it," Li added.

The Challenges Faced

Preparing for the postgraduate entrance examination was no small feat. Li had to balance his demanding job with his studies, often staying up late into the night to review textbooks and take practice tests. "It was tough, but I knew I had to push through," he said. "I had to make sacrifices, but it was worth it."

One of the biggest challenges Li faced was the language barrier. As a native Mandarin speaker, he had to adapt to the English language requirements of the postgraduate program. "I spent a lot of time practicing English, and I'm grateful for the resources available at the university," he said.

The Support System

Li's journey would not have been possible without the unwavering support of his family and friends. "My wife has been my rock throughout this journey," he said. "She encouraged me to pursue my dreams and helped me manage the household responsibilities so I could focus on my studies."

The university also played a crucial role in Li's success. "The faculty and staff were incredibly supportive," he said. "They provided me with resources and guidance whenever I needed it."

The Big Day

After months of preparation, the day of the postgraduate entrance examination finally arrived. Li felt a mix of excitement and nervousness as he walked into the exam hall. "I tried to stay calm and focused," he said. "I knew that all my hard work had led up to this moment."

When the results were announced, Li's heart raced. "I was overjoyed when I saw my name on the list of admitted students," he said. "It was a dream come true."

The Future Ahead

Li's postgraduate journey is just beginning. He plans to pursue a master's degree in education, with the ultimate goal of becoming a university professor. "I want to inspire others to pursue their dreams, just like I did," he said.

Conclusion

The story of the 41-year-old custodian from Chengdu University is a testament to the power of determination and the importance of education. Li's success serves as an inspiration to people of all ages, reminding us that it's never too late to chase our dreams. As he embarks on his postgraduate journey, Li is proof that with hard work, dedication, and support, anything is possible.
