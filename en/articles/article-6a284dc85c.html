FAQ

What is insider trading?

Insider trading refers to the practice of trading securities based on material, non-public information about a company.

How does insider trading affect the market?

Insider trading can manipulate stock prices, leading to unfair advantages for those with access to the information and potentially causing significant financial losses for other investors.
