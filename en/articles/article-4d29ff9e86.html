On a crisp autumn day, President Putin was seen waving and smiling as he left China after a successful state visit. The crowd that gathered to see him off was diverse, filled with Chinese citizens, dignitaries, and foreign journalists. As Putin approached the gates, he turned to the crowd, his expression one of genuine appreciation and warmth.

The emotional impact of Putin's farewell was palpable. Social media was flooded with videos and photos of the event, and many expressed their admiration for the Russian leader's genuine warmth.
