AI in Media: Creation, Moderation, and the Fight Against Synthetic Content

The media industry sits at the epicenter of AI's disruptive impact. AI can now write articles, generate images, compose music, and create synthetic video — all at a fraction of the cost of human creation. At the same time, media companies are deploying AI to moderate content, detect deepfakes, and optimize ad placements. The tension between AI as creator and AI as detector defines the current moment in media.

We cover the business impact of AI-generated content, the technology behind deepfake detection, the economics of AI content moderation, and the implications for trust and authenticity in media.

Key Areas We Cover

  • Deepfake Detection: The technical and economic arms race between deepfake creators and detection systems.
  • Content Moderation: How platforms use AI to police billions of pieces of content daily — and why it keeps failing.
  • Brand Safety & Ad Fraud: AI systems protecting advertiser investment from fraudulent and inappropriate content.
  • Synthetic Media & Disinformation: The threat of AI-generated disinformation campaigns and the countermeasures.
