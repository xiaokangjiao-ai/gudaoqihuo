FAQ Section

### What is Tokenomics?

Tokenomics refers to the economics of a token, including its supply, distribution, and utility. It's the system that governs how tokens are created, used, and managed within a specific ecosystem.

### How do Tokens Work in Agentic Software Engineering?

In agentic software engineering, tokens serve as a medium of exchange, a means of governance, and a tool for incentivizing user engagement. They are used to facilitate transactions, reward users, and empower them to participate in the decision-making process.

### Is Tokenomics Only Relevant to Tech-Savvy Individuals?

No, tokenomics is relevant to everyone, regardless of their technical expertise. The key is understanding how tokens can be used to create value and participate in the digital economy.
