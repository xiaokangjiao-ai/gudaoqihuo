AI in Retail: The Algorithmic Store

Retail was among the first industries to feel AI's impact — recommendation engines have driven Amazon's growth for over a decade. But the application of AI in retail has moved far beyond product recommendations. Today, AI optimizes pricing in real-time, manages inventory across thousands of SKUs, powers cashierless checkout, and personalizes every customer interaction across online and physical channels.

We analyze the real-world ROI of retail AI deployments, the technology infrastructure required, and the competitive dynamics as retailers with the best AI systems pull ahead of those without.

Key Areas We Cover

  • Recommendation & Personalization: The algorithms that drive 35% of Amazon's revenue and how they're evolving.
  • Inventory & Demand Forecasting: AI systems that reduce stockouts and overstock simultaneously.
  • Frictionless Checkout: Computer vision and sensor fusion systems eliminating the checkout line.
  • Dynamic Pricing: Real-time pricing optimization and its impact on margins and competitiveness.
