A Day in the Life: The Routine of a Cabin Crew

Cabin crew members, often affectionately called "flight attendants," undergo rigorous training to prepare for any situation that might arise during a flight. The Importance of the Work Manual

The work manual is more than just a book; it's a living document that cabin crew members rely on. For Li Jiaying, the manual is a reference tool she turns to repeatedly. Safety Protocols

The first few pages of the manual typically detail safety protocols. This includes the procedure for emergency evacuations, the location of life rafts and emergency equipment, and the correct way to administer first aid.

Customer Service Guidelines

The manual also outlines customer service guidelines, teaching cabin crew members how to handle inquiries, complaints, and special requests. It emphasizes the importance of maintaining a friendly and professional demeanor at all times.

Dietary and Religious Requirements
