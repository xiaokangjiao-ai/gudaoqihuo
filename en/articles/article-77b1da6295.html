What is Minicor?

In the bustling tech scene of China, a new startup named Minicor, which recently graduated from Y Combinator's P26 batch, has been making waves with its Windows desktop automation tool. But what's all the fuss about? Let's dive into the hype and see what the community is saying about this innovative tool.

The Buzz Around Minicor

Minicor, as described by its creators, is a "Windows desktop automation at scale." In simpler terms, it's a software that allows users to automate repetitive tasks on their Windows desktops. The idea isn't new, but the execution and the potential it offers are what's catching everyone's attention.

"Automation, Simplified"

One of the most common sentiments expressed by the community is that Minicor simplifies automation. Users are thrilled about the prospect of automating mundane tasks like file organization, system updates, and even playing a soothing sound at a specific time of the day. FAQ Section

What is Minicor?

Minicor is a Windows desktop automation tool that allows users to automate repetitive tasks on their computers.

How does Minicor work?

Minicor uses a scripting language to define and execute automation tasks. Users can create scripts to perform various actions, such as file organization, system updates, and more.

Is Minicor safe to use?

Yes, Minicor includes robust security features to prevent unauthorized access and ensure the safety of your desktop.

Can I use Minicor on a Mac?

Currently, Minicor is designed for Windows desktops. However, the team is exploring the possibility of expanding to other operating systems in the future.
