The Tech Behind the Scenes

Gemma 4 12B is a leap forward in the field of multimodal AI. Unlike previous models that required separate encoders for different types of data (like text, images, or sound), this new model can process all these inputs simultaneously without the need for additional encoding. This is a big deal because it means faster, more efficient, and more accurate processing of information.
