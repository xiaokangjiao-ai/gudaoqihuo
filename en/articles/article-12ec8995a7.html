This acquisition is no small feat. Accenture is known for its consulting services, and Ookla for its speed testing tools. When these two powerhouses combine forces, you can expect some serious moves in the tech world. Here's why it matters:

1. Combining Expertise: Accenture brings its consulting smarts, while Ookla has the tech to back it up. This could lead to innovative solutions for businesses and consumers alike.
