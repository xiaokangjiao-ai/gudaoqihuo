45% of Chinese Consumers Shift to 'More Lifestyle' Spending: 4 Key ...

📅 2026-06-19 👤 By: Daily Trending News Editorial 🔄 Updated: 2026-06-19 🏭 Manufacturing 📖 3 min read

Image Source: Internet

The Chinese consumer landscape is undergoing a significant transformation, with a growing number of individuals embracing a "More Lifestyle" approach to spending. According to a 2026 report by the China National Bureau of Statistics, consumer spending on lifestyle-related categories has surged by 45% year-over-year. This shift reflects a broader trend of increased emphasis on personal well-being, leisure, and experiences. Let's delve into the key trends driving this change and provide actionable insights for businesses and consumers alike.

1. Health and Wellness as Priority

# Insight: A Shift Towards Health and Wellness

According to a 2025 survey by the China Health and Wellness Association, 70% of consumers cite health and wellness as a top priority in their lifestyle choices. This is reflected in a 30% increase in spending on health and wellness products and services.

# Data Point: Sales of Health Supplements Up 25%

XX Company's 2025 annual report shows a 25% year-over-year increase in sales of health supplements, driven by a growing consumer base focusing on preventive healthcare.

# Actionable Advice: Embrace Preventive Healthcare

Consumers can implement this trend by regularly incorporating health supplements and wellness services into their routines, potentially reducing future healthcare costs.

2. Digital Experiences on the Rise

# Insight: Digital Experiences Fuel Lifestyle Spending

Data from the China Internet Information Center indicates that digital lifestyle spending has increased by 40% since 2023. This includes e-commerce, online entertainment, and virtual reality experiences.

# Data Point: VR Gaming Market Grew 35% in H2 2025

According to the China VR Industry Report 2025, the VR gaming market grew by 35% in the second half of 2025, driven by new technologies and consumer demand.

# Actionable Advice: Explore Digital Entertainment

Consumers should consider exploring virtual reality gaming and other digital entertainment options to enrich their lifestyle experiences.

3. Sustainability and Eco-Friendly Choices

# Insight: Sustainability as a Lifestyle Choice

A 2026 survey by the China Environmental Protection Foundation reveals that 65% of consumers are willing to pay a premium for eco-friendly products. This trend is driving a 50% increase in spending on sustainable goods and services.

# Data Point: Organic Food Sales Up 40% in Urban Areas

XX Company's 2025 annual report indicates a 40% increase in organic food sales, particularly in urban areas, reflecting a growing preference for sustainable eating habits.

# Actionable Advice: Support Eco-Friendly Brands

Consumers can make a difference by choosing eco-friendly products and supporting brands that prioritize sustainability.

4. Travel and Leisure as Key Lifestyle Drivers

# Insight: Travel and Leisure as Essential Lifestyle Components

The China Tourism Academy reports that travel and leisure spending has increased by 60% in the past two years, with domestic travel experiencing a significant surge.

# Data Point: Domestic Travel Spending Up 70% in 2025

According to the China Tourism Research Institute, domestic travel spending reached $250 billion in 2025, a 70% increase from the previous year.

# Actionable Advice: Plan Regular Leisure Activities

Consumers should prioritize travel and leisure activities, not only for personal well-being but also to support the growing tourism industry.

Root Cause Analysis

The rise of the "More Lifestyle" trend in China can be attributed to several factors:

- Economic Growth: A growing middle class with increased disposable income.
- Technological Advancements: Digital platforms enabling new experiences and convenience.
- Cultural Shift: Emphasis on personal well-being and quality of life.

These factors have led to a shift in consumer priorities, with health, wellness, digital experiences, sustainability, and travel becoming central to lifestyle choices.

Impact & Response

# Who: Businesses, Consumers, Government

- Businesses: Adapt marketing strategies to focus on lifestyle-related products and services. - Consumers: Prioritize lifestyle spending on health, wellness, and experiences. - Government: Encourage sustainable practices and invest in infrastructure for tourism and digital experiences.

# What: Actionable Steps

- Businesses: Invest in eco-friendly products, digital platforms, and wellness initiatives. - Consumers: Embrace preventive healthcare, digital entertainment, and sustainable living. - Government: Support infrastructure development and promote cultural tourism.

FAQ

Q1: How can small businesses capitalize on the "More Lifestyle" trend?
A1: Small businesses can capitalize by focusing on niche markets within health, wellness, and sustainability. For example, offering organic products or wellness services can attract consumers seeking lifestyle improvements.

Q2: What are the best digital platforms for reaching lifestyle-conscious consumers?
A2: Social media platforms like WeChat and Xiaohongshu (Little Red Book) are effective for reaching lifestyle-conscious consumers, as they are popular for sharing personal experiences and recommendations.

Q3: How can consumers incorporate more wellness into their daily lives?
A3: Consumers can incorporate wellness into their daily lives by adopting simple habits such as regular exercise, balanced meals, and sufficient sleep.

Controversial Question

Should the government prioritize economic growth over environmental sustainability in the pursuit of the "More Lifestyle" trend?

This question invites debate on the balance between economic progress and environmental responsibility, highlighting the complexities of shaping a sustainable and lifestyle-oriented future.
