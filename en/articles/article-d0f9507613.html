Keyword Filters

#### Understanding the Great Firewall
The Great Firewall is a system that monitors, filters, and blocks foreign websites deemed harmful or politically sensitive. It's a lot like a bouncer for the Chinese internet, only with a lot more technology and a stricter blacklist.

#### Common Blocked Sites
Popular Western platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Google, and YouTube are just a few examples of sites that get the boot. However, the list of blocked content is extensive and includes anything from political commentary to foreign news sources.

Accessing Chinese Websites

#### Domestic Platforms Over International Ones
When in China, you'll notice a preference for domestic websites over international ones. For instance, WeChat is the king of communication apps, and Baidu is the go-to search engine. These platforms are tailored to the local audience and offer features that may not be available elsewhere.

#### Cyberspace Regulations
Websites operating within China must follow strict regulations, including content censorship and data retention policies. This means that any foreign company looking to set up shop needs to comply with these rules or risk being shut down.

Navigating the Digital Divide

#### Using VPNs and Proxy Servers
For those desperate to access foreign websites, VPNs and proxy servers can be your saving grace. These tools mask your IP address and route your internet traffic through different servers, effectively bypassing the Great Firewall.

#### Risks and Limitations
While VPNs are a popular workaround, they're not foolproof. The Chinese government has cracked down on VPN usage, and there are always risks involved, from security concerns to legal issues.

### Can I access all international websites in China?

No, many international websites are blocked, including popular social media platforms and search engines.

While VPNs are technically legal, their usage has been restricted in recent years, and authorities have cracked down on VPN providers.

Using a VPN can lead to fines, legal action, or even imprisonment. It's essential to understand the risks involved.

### Can I set up a business in China without using a domestic platform?

It's possible, but it will be challenging to reach your target audience without using domestic platforms like WeChat and Baidu.
