About AI Verticals

AI Verticals is a specialized publication covering the intersection of artificial intelligence and industry.

Our Mission

We track how AI is reshaping eight major sectors: Finance, Healthcare, Legal, Education, Manufacturing, Retail, HR, and Media. Our analysis is grounded in real company data, market research, and expert commentary - not hype.

What We Cover

From fraud detection algorithms in banking to predictive maintenance on factory floors, we document the applications that matter. Every article includes specific data points, named companies, and verifiable sources.

Editorial Standards

Our content follows strict editorial guidelines. Articles must include real-world case studies, quantitative data, and actionable insights. We do not publish press releases or sponsored content without clear disclosure.
