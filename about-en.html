About Us

Welcome to Daily Trending News (gudaoqihuo.com) — an AI-assisted intelligent news platform.

Our Mission

In an era of information overload, we are committed to using AI technology to quickly aggregate, organize, and interpret global trending topics, helping you stay informed in the shortest time possible. Our content covers six major categories: Trending News, Technology, Health, Lifestyle, Entertainment, and Finance, updated daily in both Chinese and English.

Content Production

Our content production process:

  1. Trending Topic Aggregation: Daily automated collection from Baidu, Weibo, Toutiao, Zhihu, Reddit, Hacker News, and other platforms
  2. AI-Assisted Creation: Large language models (GLM-4) perform deep analysis and content generation on trending topics
  3. Human Review: We review generated content and perform necessary manual edits to ensure quality and compliance
  4. Automated Publishing: Deployed to this website via GitHub Actions, ensuring stable daily updates

We firmly believe that AI is a tool, not the destination. Our goal is to use AI to improve content production efficiency, but the final judgment and choices always remain in your hands.

⚠️ Content Transparency Disclosure

  • We do not fully rely on AI output; human judgment is always retained

Editorial Principles

📌 Truth First: We cite verifiable sources whenever possible, and do not fabricate or exaggerate.

📌 Multiple Perspectives: For the same event, we strive to present viewpoints from different angles.

📌 Long-term Value: We chase not only traffic, but also content with long-term reference value for readers.

📌 Continuous Evolution: We are constantly learning and improving. If you have suggestions, please feel free to contact us.
